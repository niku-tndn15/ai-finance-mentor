# UrPaisa PRD Source of Truth

This folder is the product source of truth for UrPaisa.

UrPaisa is an AI-native personal finance mentor. It helps users understand their money situation, know what is safe to spend, take one clear action, build better habits, and improve over time.

Core loop:

`Know your money situation -> get one clear action -> act on it -> build better habits -> improve over time`

## Document Map

1. `00-master-product-prd.md` - Product vision, user problem, positioning, scope, and success metrics.
2. `01-mvp-experience-prd.md` - First-time, daily, weekly, and monthly user journeys for MVP.
3. `02-financial-situation-model-prd.md` - Income, fixed expense, goal, debt, and safe-spend models.
4. `03-safe-to-use-money-prd.md` - Safe-to-use money calculation, safe spend meter, and risk zones.
5. `04-goal-and-debt-coach-prd.md` - Goal coaching and debt payoff guidance.
6. `05-nudge-and-recommendation-engine-prd.md` - Behavioral nudges and next-best-action logic.
7. `06-habit-learning-loop-prd.md` - Habit model, action compliance, adaptive tone, and AI learning.
8. `07-conversational-mentor-prd.md` - Natural-language money questions and AI Financial Twin direction.
9. `08-reflections-resets-challenges-prd.md` - Weekly reflection, monthly reset, and habit challenges.
10. `09-data-privacy-ai-safety-prd.md` - Privacy, financial safety, AI safety, and disclaimers.
11. `10-roadmap-advanced-features-prd.md` - Advanced features after MVP validation.

## Source-of-Truth Rule

If future prompts, designs, or implementation plans conflict with these PRDs, these PRDs win unless the product owner explicitly updates them.
