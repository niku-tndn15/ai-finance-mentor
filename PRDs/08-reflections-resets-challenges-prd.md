# UrPaisa Reflections, Resets, and Challenges PRD

## 1. Objective

Help users turn actions into habits through weekly reflection, monthly reset, and small habit challenges.

## 2. Weekly Money Reflection

Weekly reflection should feel like a mentor-style check-in, not a boring report.

It includes:

- What went well.
- What did not go well.
- Pattern detected.
- One recommended action.
- Updated money health signal or habit insight.
- Progress toward goals.

Example:

"This week, you completed 4 out of 6 money actions. You were strong on bill payments but skipped savings twice. Your best next move is to save INR 800 right after your next payday."

## 3. Monthly Money Reset

Monthly reset helps users plan the next month.

It answers:

- Did I overspend?
- Did I save enough?
- Which category or behavior hurt me most?
- Which goal moved forward?
- What should I change next month?
- What is my safe-to-use money for next month?

Example:

"Last month, eating out was your biggest flexible expense. Reducing it by 20 percent next month can free INR 1,800 for your emergency fund."

MVP can support a lightweight monthly reset based on manual inputs and nudge history. Advanced reset can use transaction data later.

## 4. Habit Challenges

UrPaisa can create small financial challenges.

Examples:

- Save INR 100 daily for 7 days.
- No food delivery for 3 days.
- Cancel one unused subscription.
- Move INR 500 to emergency fund.
- Spend under INR 1,000 this weekend.
- Pay one bill before due date.
- Wait 24 hours before buying non-essential items.

Each challenge has:

- Difficulty level.
- Expected impact.
- Completion tracking.
- Reward message.
- Habit score improvement.

## 5. Challenge Recommendation Rules

Recommend challenges based on:

- Current financial risk.
- User goal.
- Habit weakness.
- Past completion behavior.
- User readiness.

Examples:

- If user skips big savings nudges, suggest INR 100 daily challenge.
- If user overspends weekends, suggest weekend limit challenge.
- If subscriptions are high, suggest subscription cleanup challenge.

## 6. MVP Requirements

MVP must include:

- Weekly reflection.
- One suggested habit focus for next week.

MVP may include:

- Simple optional challenge.

MVP can defer:

- Formal challenge library.
- Money Health Score updates.
- Monthly reset automation.

## 7. Acceptance Criteria

- Weekly reflection uses actual action history.
- Reflection includes one next action.
- User can understand progress without charts.
- Optional challenge is small and measurable.
- Monthly reset remains consistent with safe-to-use money and goals.
