# UrPaisa Goal and Debt Coach PRD

## 1. Objective

Help users make progress toward goals and manage debt using small, clear actions.

UrPaisa should guide the user on whether to save first, pay debt first, or balance both.

## 2. Goal Coach

Each goal should have:

- Goal type.
- Target amount.
- Current savings.
- Deadline.
- Priority.
- Flexibility.
- Monthly contribution needed.
- Progress percentage.

Goal actions:

- Save first on payday.
- Move extra cash at week end.
- Reduce one non-essential expense.
- Use bonus income toward the goal.
- Protect goal savings from casual spending.

Example:

"You are 22 percent done with your emergency fund. If you save INR 1,500 this week, you will stay on track."

## 3. Goal Priority Rules

Default priority:

1. Emergency fund.
2. High-interest debt repayment.
3. Essential upcoming obligations.
4. Time-bound goals.
5. Lifestyle goals.
6. Long-term investing readiness.

The user can override priority.

## 4. Debt Payoff Coach

UrPaisa captures:

- Debt type.
- Outstanding amount.
- Interest rate.
- Minimum payment.
- Due date.
- Late fee risk.
- Emotional stress level.

Debt strategies:

- Avalanche: pay high-interest debt first.
- Snowball: pay smaller debts first for motivation.
- Hybrid: pay urgent dues first, then high-interest debt.

Example:

"You have INR 3,000 extra this month. Paying your credit card first saves more interest than paying your education loan early."

## 5. Debt Urgency Rules

Highest urgency:

- Payment due soon.
- High late fee risk.
- High-interest credit card debt.
- BNPL or short-term dues with penalties.

Medium urgency:

- Personal loan.
- Informal borrowing with emotional pressure.

Lower urgency:

- Lower-interest education or vehicle loans, unless due soon.

## 6. Save vs Debt Guidance

UrPaisa should consider:

- Emergency fund status.
- Debt interest rate.
- Due date.
- Late fee risk.
- User stress level.
- Safe-to-use money.

Default behavior:

- If no emergency fund and high-interest debt exists, balance minimum emergency buffer with debt payoff.
- If payment due soon, prioritize avoiding late fees.
- If high-interest debt has no immediate due date, recommend extra payment when safe-to-use money allows.

## 7. MVP Requirements

MVP must support:

- At least one goal.
- At least one debt item.
- Required monthly goal contribution.
- Debt minimum payment and due date.
- Simple save-vs-debt recommendation.

MVP can defer:

- Detailed amortization.
- Product recommendations.
- Loan refinancing advice.

## 8. Acceptance Criteria

- User can add a goal and see progress.
- User can add debt and see urgency.
- UrPaisa can recommend one goal or debt action.
- Guidance avoids regulated investment or credit advice.
- User can mark goal/debt actions Done, Skip, or Remind me later.
